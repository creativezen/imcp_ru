Metaсognitive Programming (MCP) is a coaching methodology created by Misha Saidov (more about him here) that has been demonstrated to be effective for a range of problems andlife dilemmas.

MCP is a tool that helps you identify your originalprogramming and rewire yourself.

It combines powerful tools from psychology, biology,neuroscience, psychotherapy, and philosophy, focusing on helping people accept what theycannot change and changing what they should not accept.

MCP combines evidence-basedtools of psychological intervention that lead to improvement in functioning and quality of lifeand are supported by numerous research studies.
