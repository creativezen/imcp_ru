This approach has repeatedly proved itself, helping people break down their barriers and bridging the gap between the present and the desired future.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Misha Saidov, The Institute of Metacognitive Programming (IMCP) emerged froma realization of the potential in coaching and the limitations of conventional methodologies.

This realization fueled Misha's determination to pioneera coaching method that births meaningful transformation - Metacognitive Programming (MCP).

With a focus on reshaping mindsets and guiding individuals toward lasting personal evolution, IMCP has evolved into a trailblazer in the field of coaching and psychotherapy. What started as a vision has blossomed into a globally recognized institution, empowering thousands of individuals and coaches to discover their true potential and achieve unparalleled personal and professional growth.

At IMCP, our narrative is one of continuous innovation, unwavering dedication, and a relentless drive to elevate the human experience for individuals and communities worldwide.

Our Mission

At IMCP, our unwavering mission stands tall: to elevate the quality of human experience for one billion individuals worldwide.

We passionately pursue this goal through transformative coaching, aiming to empower, inspire, and facilitate positive change in the lives of individuals globally. Our commitment extends beyond conventional boundaries, striving to truly enrich lives, foster personal growth, and unleash the boundless potential within each individual, one transformative coaching session at a time.

Our Vision

Founded on an audacious goal to elevate the quality of human experience for one billion people, we envision a world where as many people as possible are able to unlock their greatest potential through transformative coaching.

Our vision is rooted in the belief that enlightened individuals have the capacity to uplift others, that’s why we have committed ourselves to guiding individuals to self-discovery.
